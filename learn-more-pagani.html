About Pagani

Pagani is an Italian manufacturer of high-performance sports cars. Founded in 1992 by Horacio Pagani, the company is known for its exquisite craftsmanship and engineering precision. Pagani cars are designed to be works of art on wheels, combining cutting-edge technology with luxurious materials.

Horacio Pagani

Horacio Pagani is the visionary founder and creator of Pagani Automobili S.p.A. With a passion for engineering and design, he has pushed the boundaries of automotive innovation. His dedication to perfection is reflected in every Pagani vehicle, making them true masterpieces.

Pagani Huayra R

The Pagani Huayra R is a limited-production track-focused hypercar. It features a naturally aspirated V12 engine that produces an astonishing amount of horsepower, making it one of the most powerful cars ever built. The Huayra R is a true masterpiece of automotive engineering.
